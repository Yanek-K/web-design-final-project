    Three Websites for downloads

    1. PNG Wing

      Offers a wide range of free, high-quality PNG images with transparent backgrounds, perfect for design projects.

    2. Icons 8

      Provides a vast collection of free, customizable icons in various formats for different platforms and design needs.

    3. Pixabay

      A great resource for free sound effects, offering a variety of high-quality audio clips for creative projects.

    4. Pexels

      Features an extensive library of free videos, including niche topics like darkroom printing, ideal for visual storytelling.

    Three Websites for technical information

    1. MDN Web Docs

      I appreciate how in-depth and reliable the information is, especially with the detailed examples and compatibility notes across different browsers.

    2. W3schools

      I like how simple and easy it is to follow, with interactive examples that make learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript really accessible.

    3. Geeks for Geeks

      I enjoy how it combines both fundamental and advanced topics, providing clear explanations along with real-world problem-solving examples.
